Supported Ad Formats

JW Platform offers support for 4 different ad configurations:

VAST/VPAID Pre-roll
This sets a single pre-roll ad, served by a VAST-compliant video ad server (nearly any ad server today). See our VAST/VPAID blog post for some background info.
Google IMA Pre-roll
This sets a single pre-roll ad, served through the Interactive Media Ads (IMA) SDK version 3. LongTail Video is one of Google's official video technology partners.
VMAP Ad Schedule
This sets a full ad schedule, which can consist of multiple pre-, mid- and post-roll ad breaks. Since VMAP is a fairly new standard, not many video ad servers yet support it. Google's Dart For Publishers (DFP) is one of the adservers that does support VMAP.
VAST Tag with Fallbacks(Waterfall)
This option allows for the configuration of up to three VAST ad tags. JW Platform will then attempt to play each of these tags, in order, until an impression is delivered. If all three tags fail to deliver an impression, your video content will begin. For more information regarding VAST tag waterfalling, see our document here.

See the Supported Ad Formats article for more details, a list of verified VAST ad servers and a list of supported VAST tag variables.

Advertising Setup

All advertising options are set in the Advertising tab of the player setup pages in the dashboard:

In addition to providing the URL to your VAST, IMA or VMAP tag, you can set the following properties:

  • A custom Ad Message, displayed in the controlbar during ad playback.
  • The ability to Skip Ads after a certain amount of seconds. This enables you to balance the interests of advertisers and viewers.
  • The location to insert a Companion Ad into. Companions are only inserted when the ad tag has a creative that matches the provided dimensions.

For VMAP schedules, JW Player will automatically render Ad Markers on the controlbar, to signal to viewers where mid-roll ad breaks are located.

 

Key Value Pairs with VAST or Googima Tags

Key Value pairs within JW Platform require two pieces to be configured within our platform. These are:

  1. Metadata for video objects to associate key words with videos
  2. Custom ad tag variables to reference the metadata in item 1
