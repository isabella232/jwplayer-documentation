Sharing of videos through Facebook, Twitter, blogs and email can dramatically increase reach across the web. See this blog post on sharing for more info and some hints.

JW Platform contains point & click social sharing functionality. This includes functionality playback of your videos inside the Facebook wall and Twitter cards. This document explains what the various options are, and what works best in a situation.

This document applies to users of JW Platform. Don't have Hosting? Upgrade Now.

Sharing Setup

Getting started is quite easy. Simply go to the dashboard "players" tab and click on a player to edit it. In the general section of the player properties you will find the Sharing dropdown selector:

When sharing is enabled, an icon appears in the top-left corner of the player. This icon can be used by your viewers to bring up the social sharing overlay.

Sharing Overlay

The Social Sharing overlay for JW Player contains several options to share a video:

  • Share with Facebook. The video will play inside the Facebook wall.
  • Share with Twitter. The video will play inside the Twitter feed.
  • Share with email. This will popup a new email with a link to the video.
  • Copy/paste the video link to share using any other service.
  • Copy/paste the embed code to embed the video in blogs.
