This article applies to customers on the JW Platform dashboard under the Pay-as-you-go billing model.


Do I have to Migrate to the new dashboard?

Yes. We will sunset the JW Platform dashboard on March 1st, 2016. The new dashboard retains all existing functionality though, plus adds a host of new features (like deeper analytics, management of external URLs and multiple users, and access to player downloads).

Moving to our new dashboard will not affect your content delivery, in-progress transcodes, API integrations or billing. Only the user interface will be updated (instantly and seamlessly).  

 

Do I have to migrate to the new billing model?

Yes. We will stop accepting new Pay-as-you-go payments on March 1st, 2016. At that date, we will also sunset the JW Platform dashboard and migrate its users to our new JW Player dashboard.

For typical Pay-as-you-go usage, we now offer the JW Player Premium edition. Priced at $299/year, it includes 50GB storage forever and 250GB streaming per month. Depending upon your usage volumes, this is 2 to 10 times cheaper than your existing Pay-as-you go pricing! On top of that, we offer a 50% discount if you have an outstanding storage or streaming balance.

If you stream and/or store more than the amounts included with JW Player Premium, we will contact you with a custom offer. Alternatively, feel free to contact us for more info.

 

If I migrate to JW Player Free, can I later upgrade to Premium?

Yes. You can upgrade to JW Player Premium at any time. Do note the 50% discount we offer current Pay-as-you-go customers will expire after March 1, 2016.
