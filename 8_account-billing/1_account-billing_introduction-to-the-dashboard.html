We're excited to introduce you to the JW Player Dashboard! You'll find all the functionality you need in a clean interface that makes your job easier.

The below is a step-by-step tour of your new dashboard. If you have any questions or concerns, post a question in the forum, contact our support team (JW Player Premium and higher only), or reach out to your account representative.

 

 

 

 

Dashboard Homepage

The dashboard homepage contains links to access important tools, like:

  • Player & SDK downloads and license keys
  • Blog posts about latest features and updates
  • Support Articles and link to Contact Support

It also contains information about new product releases, information about your account's usage (for Admin users only), and the Quick Publish tool.

Quick Publish Tool

The Quick Publish Tool enables you to add content and get embed code right away via the Publish Mode.

Quick Publish accepts two types of content: Uploads, which can be added via a filepicker or drag & drop, and externally-hosted URLs, which can be added via copy & paste.

For information about adding files directly to the library (without publishing right away), read our article about uploading content.

 

Content

Library and Playlists

Under the content section you will find your video library and your playlists. You can add videos (or audio) by uploading files or adding external URLs.

 

Content Detail

Add tags, metadata, tracks and view sources for your video. Within the tracks tab, add and edit caption files using our captions editor tool.

 

Publish Mode

You will notice the Publish Mode widget at the top of your Homepage, Content and Players List pages in the dashboard. Each “Embed” link or button opens Publish Mode, where you can find a single-line embed code for the player + content combination that you have selected.

From here, you can:

  1. Click the "change" button next to the Player or Content to swap with other existing Players or Content
  2. Select "javascript" (recommended) or "iframe" for your embed type
  3. Include schema.org SEO tags — this automatically includes text from your video metadata in your embed code
  4. Click the "preview page" link to see a preview of your embed

 

 

Players

Create new players and edit existing ones in the Players section of your dashboard. All JW Player accounts come with two example players: one responsive and one fixed-width.

The player list gives you an overview of all of your players including a preview of their skins, their size, and their version numbers.

Your License Keys & Downloads can also be accessed from this page.

Clicking on a player title in the players list will open the detail page for that player.

Here, you can update settings for playback, design, sharing, analytics, and more.

On this page you'll also notice the Player Library URL tool — this gives you quick access to the library URL for this cloud player and its settings. Click here to learn more about using player libraries.

 

Analytics

Monitor your content performance, and look at activity by device, geography, engagement, and more. Player activity is associated with a unique media ID, to ensure that everything is attributed accurately. Read more about Media IDs, using the new analytics, and most importantly what's new in Analytics as compared to your legacy JW Platform dashboard.

 

Account

In the Account menu you will find access to:

  • Your user Profile
  • Properties management
  • User management
  • Usage analytics
  • Billing information
  • Sign out of your account
Note: Manager users will only see the "profile" and "sign out" options.
