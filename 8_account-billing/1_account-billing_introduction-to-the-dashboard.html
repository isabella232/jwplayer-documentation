We're excited to introduce you to the JW Player Dashboard! You'll find all the functionality you need in a clean interface that makes your job easier.

 

 

 

 

 

Here's a step-by-step tour of your new dashboard. If you have any questions or concerns, post a question in the forum, contact our support team (JW Player Premium and higher only), or reach out to your account representative.

Dashboard Homepage

The dashboard homepage contains links to access important tools, like:

  • Player & SDK downloads and license keys
  • Blog posts about latest features and updates
  • Support Articles and link to Contact Support


It also contains information about new product releases, information about your account's usage (for Admin users only), and the Quick Publish tool.

Quick Publish Tool

The Quick Publish Tool enables you to quickly add an upload (by drag & drop) or URL (by copy & paste). For information about adding files in bulk, skip to the content section of this page.


Content

Video Library and Playlists

Under the content section you will find your video library and your playlists. You can add videos (or audio) by uploading files or adding external URLs.

Content Detail

Add tags, metadata, tracks and view sources for your video. Within the tracks tab, add and edit caption files using our captions editor tool.

Publish Mode

On pages from which you can embed players or content, you will notice the Publish Mode widget at the top of the page. Each “Embed” link or button will open Publish Mode to create your single line embed code. Additionally, if you are embedding a single video, you can choose to include schema.org SEO tags which automatically pulls from your video metadata. You can also use Publish Mode to preview content using the preview page link.

Analytics

Monitor your content performance, and look at activity by device, geography, engagement, and more. Player activity is associated with a unique media ID, to ensure that everything is attributed accurately. Read more about Media IDs, using the new analytics, and most importantly what's new in Analytics as compared to your JW Platform dashboard.


Account Menu

In the Account menu you will find access to:

  • Your user profile
  • Properties management
  • User management
  • Usage analytics
  • Billing information
