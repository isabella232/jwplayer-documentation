It is very important to make sure your live stream is the highest possible quality. Make sure you choose your video/audio settings from our recommended settings below, which will result in a reliable stream based on your Internet connection.

It is important to test your workflow before going live. We recommend conducting a full workflow test from beginning to end. Tests should include the actual camera(s), and other hardware/software you'll be using in your production workflow. Also, the test should be conducted for the entire length of your expected broadcast length to ensure reliability.

NOTE: Please also make sure that you test your internet connection prior to broadcasting to ensure adequate throughput. It is recommended to have at least 3x the bitrate of uplink bandwidth for a stable connection.

Resolution & Bitrate

The following formats, frame rates, and resolutions are supported by JW Live. Please use the recommended settings below for optimal performance.

Format Frame Rate Resolution Bitrate
1080p (H.264) 60p 1920 x 1080 7Mbps - 9Mbps
1080p (H.264) 24p/30p 1920 x 1080 5Mbps - 6Mbps

720p (H.264) 24p/30p 1280 x 720 3Mbps - 4Mbps
480p (H.264) 24p/30p 854 x 480 1Mbps - 2Mbps
 

Encoder settings

Protocol: RTMP
Video codec: H.264, 4.1
Number of tracks: Single video and audio track supported (no multitrack sources)
Frame rate: up to 60 fps (no variable framerate)
Keyframe frequency:

2 seconds (do not exceed 4 seconds)
Audio codec: AAC or MP3
Bitrate encoding: CBR (NO variable bitrate)


Recommended advanced settings

Pixel aspect ratio: Square
Frame types: Progressive Scan, 2 B-Frames, 1 Reference Frame
Entropy coding: CABAC
Audio sample rate: 44.1 KHz
Audio bitrate: 128 Kbps stereo
