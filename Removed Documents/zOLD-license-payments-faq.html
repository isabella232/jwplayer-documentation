Do I need to purchase a license?

Below is a list of the most common reasons you will need to purchase a JW Player license:

  • You are interested in one or more of the features offered in our licensed player editions.
  • You exceed, or expect to exceed, our monthly video play quota.
  • You would like to run ads in your JW Player to monetize your video content.

How do I purchase a license?

To purchase JW Player, visit our Pricing Page to view our different editions and offerings. If you're choosing the Premium edition, you can order immediately. Enterprise and hosting upgrades are handled via our Contact Us page. Note: If you have an existing Free account with us, make sure that you are using the same email address when upgrading.

If I wish to run ads via a third party plugin, do I still need an Ads license?

To run video ads in any form, you must be properly licensed with an Ad version.

I've just upgraded my license. Why is my player not reflecting this change?

After upgrading your license, the key found on your account page will change to reflect this. As a result, you will need to manually update your self-hosted key on your website. If a cloud-hosted library was utilized, this change will happen automatically.

How many domains can be used with a single JW7 license?

As with JW6, Free users can use their player on one domain, when JW Player Premium can be used on up to 10 domains. For more domains, you will need to utilize an Enterprise License.

What payment methods do you accept?

When purchasing online, we accept only credit card and PayPal.

Where can I change my payment information?

Billing information can be changed on your billing page. We will reference this updated information on your next billing cycle.

I am interested in an Enterprise or Ads license. Also, video hosting! How do I purchase any of these?

Enterprise/Ads licenses, as well as hosting upgrades, are handled via our Contact Us page. A sales team member will be in contact shortly after submitting.

I am interested in reselling JW Player, how do I find more information?

If you are interested in becoming a reseller for JW Player, please Contact Us directly.

Can I transfer my license to someone else?

JW Player licenses are non-transferrable.

What is your refund policy?

All fees are non-refundable except if otherwise provided in the Terms of Service.

I seem to be getting an error while trying to pay with my American Express credit card.

American Express cards are unique and require a 4 digit security code located on the front of your credit card. Make sure you use this code, instead of a 3 digit code.
