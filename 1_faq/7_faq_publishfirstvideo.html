Welcome! We're excited to help you get started — JW Player makes it easy to publish a video that will play everywhere. This simple tutorial will walk you through embedding your first video with JW Player.

Before you start, prepare your content. You can publish a file from (1) a URL, or (2) from your machine.

 
  1. Sign in to your JW Player dashboard
  2. On your dashboard homepage, locate the Quick Publish tool and select which type of content you are publishing (see diagram): (1) URL or (2) Upload
  3. Input your content of the selected type in the Quick Publish tool
  4. Publish Mode will open with a default player and your embed code ready to go
  5. Copy the embed code from Publish Mode, and paste it into your webpage's code — and you're all set!

 

Player Tip: If you'd like to customize a player first, click through to the "Players" section and create a new player, or make changes to an existing one. Then, click "embed" on that player from the Player List to grab your embed code.

Content Tip: We keep track of all the content you add through the dashboard. If you'd like to edit the title, description, or poster image, assign tags, or even add more content & more, navigate to the Content section.
