Introduction

JW Platform allows you to create multiple templates of JW Player. Each template contains settings for player layout, playback behaviour (e.g. autostart) video advertising and 3rd party integrations. Whenever you publish a video or playlist, you select a player template to use. Here are two player examples:

Any updates you make to your players are reflected for videos already published with this player. This setup makes players a powerful tool to e.g. change advertising options or site design without updating all individual embed codes.

Editing Players

New JW Platform accounts already have some preset templates. You can choose to edit these or create new players with the Create New Player button in the players overview page. Both actions direct you to the player properties page:

This page shows a preview of your player template, with all properties grouped in 5 sections below:

  • General: Set the player's name, as well as its dimensions and skin.
  • Layout: Show or hide the controls, the playlist and a custom logo.
  • Playback: Set playback options like autostart, repeating and streaming.
  • Advertising: Insert IAB or IMA ads and control their user experience.
  • Integrations: Connect to Google/Adobe Analytics and set Sharing/Related overlays.

Most properties are available to all JW Platform customers, but the Advertising section and SiteCatalyst integration are only available if you have also purchased JW Ads. Please contact your account representative for more info.

Player Properties

Nearly all player properties displayed in the dashboard map directly to JW Player configuration options. Only the following properties need some further explanation:

  • Social Sharing goes beyond the overlay, enabling inline Facebook/Twitter playback.
  • Related Videos are automatically calculated based upon your video's tags.
  • Advertising is incredibly straightforward to setup and modify with JW Platform.
  • Video Streaming allows you to disable adaptive in favor of your original uploads.
