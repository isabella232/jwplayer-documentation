This article applies to all users except JW Platform users.

Your JW Player Dashboard comes complete with an analytics section, where you can monitor your content performance, and look at activity by device, geography, engagement, and more. Player activity is associated with a unique media ID, to ensure that everything is attributed accurately. Click here to learn more about Media IDs.


How to Ensure Data Capture

Note: If you already use the dashboard to add content and generate embed code, your setup work is already done.

  1. Get Media IDs for your content via the Dashboard or the API
    • Dashboard: Add externally-hosted URLs or upload your content via the dashboard, and find the Media ID on each piece of content’s detail page
      • Go to your Content section, and select “upload” or “add via URL” on the big action button on the top left
    • API: The /videos/create method supports externally-hosted URLs

  2. Embed all content using Media IDs
    • Dashboard: Once your content is added, click “embed” on a video from the detail page or list page. (Publish Mode generates single-line embed codes that contain a reference to your content’s Media ID.)
    • API: Include the Media ID parameter in your player embeds, as in the below code example: 
      playlist:[{
file:"",
mediaid:"eXaMPlE1"
}]


What are the metrics shown? 

 
Content: Any video or audio content object with a unique Media ID

Embeds: Players that have loaded on a webpage

Plays: Video starts across all embedded players

Completes: Videos watched to completion

Daily Unique Viewers: Unique users who have played at least one video

Plays per Unique: The number of plays divided by the number of unique viewers on specific day

Complete Rate: The number of plays divided by the number of completes, as a percentage

Time Watched: Total time watched across all your players

Engagement: Use this data to track the performance of your video (where do viewers drop off? which sections do they replay?)
